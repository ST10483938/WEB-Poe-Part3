Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to strengthen food security, build skills, and empower our community

Community Gardening

Our flagship program brings neughbors together to grow fresh vegitables and herbs for local families. Through Collaborative farming, we've building food security while strengthening community bonds

What We Offer:

  • Individual and shared plot allocations
  • Organic seeds and seedlings
  • Garden tools and equipment access
  • Harvesting and distribution coorination
  • Seasonal crop rotetion planning
Schedule:Monday-Saturday, 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Location: Main Gardern Plots, Khayelitsha
Cost: Free for community members

Educational Workshops

Knowledge-sharing sessions that teach sustainable farming techniques, nutrition education, and food preservation skills to empower community members with lifelong capabiliities.

Workshop Topics:

  • Composting and soil health management
  • Water conservation and irrigation methods
  • Pest management using natural methods
  • Nutrition and healthy cooking classes
  • Food preservation and storage techniques
  • Small-scale farming for income generation
Schedule:Saturdays, 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Duration: 3-hour sessions
Languages: English, Xhosa, Afrkaans

Youth Development program

Engaging you people aged 12-25 in agriculture and environmental stewardship through hand-on learning, mentorship, and leadership development opportunities.

Program Components:

  • After-school gardening clubs
  • Environmental awareness workshops
  • Youth leadership training
  • Entrepreneurship in agriculture
  • Mentorship with community leeaders
  • Annual youth garden competition
Age Group: 12-25 years old
Schedule: Weekdays after school, weekends
Capacity: 30 youth participants per semester

Nutrition and Health Program

Comprehensive health education focusing on the connection between fresh produce and community wellness, including cooking classes and nutritional counseling

Health Initiatives:

  • Nutritional assessment and counseling
  • Healthy cooking demonstrations
  • Traditional food preparation methods
  • Community health screenings
  • Food safety and hygiene training
  • Recipe sharing and meal planning
Target:Families and individuals of all ages
Frequency: Monthly sessions
Partners: Local healthcare providers
