Getting Here

Public Transport:

  • Golden Arrow Bus: Take route 142 or 143 to Khayelitsha
  • Taxi: Khayelitsha taxi route from Cape Town CBD
  • Train: Khayelitsha Station (10-minute walk to garden)

By Car:

  • Take N2 highway toward Somerset West
  • Exit at Khayelitsha/Baden Powell Drive
  • Follow signs to Site B, Khayelitsha
  • Turn left on Ntlazane Road
  • Garden entrance on the right at corner with Spine Road

Parking:

Free parking available on-site. Security present during operating hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit without an appointment?

Yes! We welcome drop-in visitors during our regular garden hours. However, if you'd like a guided tour or want to speak with specific staff members, we recommend calling ahead.

How do I sign up for workshops?

You can register for workshops by calling our education coordinator at +27 21 123 4568 or filling out the volunteer form on our Get Involved page.

Do you offer programs in local languages?

Yes! Many of our programs are offered in English, Xhosa, and Afrikaans. Our staff and volunteers are multilingual and can provide translation when needed.

Can schools bring students for educational visits?

Absolutely! We love hosting school groups. Please contact our education coordinator at least two weeks in advance to schedule group visits and educational programs.

Is there a minimum age for volunteering?

Volunteers under 18 are welcome but must have parental consent. We have specific youth volunteer programs for ages 12-17, and different opportunities for adult volunteers.

How can I make a donation?

You can donate online through our Get Involved page, or contact us for information about in-kind donations of tools, seeds, or materials.
