About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the dedicated team working to transform our community

Our Story

Khanya's Community Garden began in 2018 when local resident Khanya Mthembu noticed the growing food insecurity in our Khayelitsha commmunity. What started as a small vegetable patch on unused municipal land has blossmed into a thriving 2-hectare community hub that now serves over 300 families

Driven by the belief that everyone deserves access to fresh, nutritious food, Khanya rallied neighbors to tranform empty lots into productive gardens. Through collective effort, we've created not just a source of food,but a space where community bonds are strengthened and skills are shared.

Our Mission

To combatfood insecurity, promotr sustainable agriculture, and strengthen community bonds through Collaborative gardening, education, and skill Development programs.

Our Values

  • Community empowerment
  • Environmental sustainablility
  • Food security for all
  • Collaborative learning
  • Cultural respect
